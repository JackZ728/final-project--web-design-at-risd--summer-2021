About me

My name is Jack Zhou. I am an illustration student currently studying at the Rhode Island School of Design. I am an aspiring illustrator or concept artist for the film and game industry. I was born in China, but quickly immigrated to Canada, where I grew up and currently live. I love visual storytelling, and love creating narratives through my drawings and paintings.

I primarily work in digital media, using programs like photoshop or procreate to create digital paintings and designs. My work revolves around creating fantasy worlds, and populating those worlds with fantastical characters. I am still on my journey to improve my skills and my ability to create worlds.
